Our Donate Plan

We want to distribute food among the helpless people there. We want to go to the marginalized people where food aid has not yet reached. We want to distribute food among those people and make them smile.

Food For A Family

We will distribute food there in the form of family packages. For a family there will be five kg of potatoes, ten kg of rice, five kg of oil, two kg of pulses.

Shelter For A Family

We will select a high place there and arrange for the flood victims to stay. We will build a shelter that can accommodate at least 1000 people. The biggest challenge for flood victims is toilet management. We will arrange some semi-finished toilets there considering their good health.

Education For Child

As all the schools and colleges are closed during the flood, we will run temporary education programs for the younger children in morning and evening shifts.
